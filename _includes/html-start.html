Testing Kernagic

Automated spacing of digital fonts
https://github.com/hodefoting/kernagic

Kernagic is software under development to automate the metrics spacing of digital fonts.

(below) opening UFO file in Kernagic.

(below) First, a 'good' sense of spacing and rhythm is achieved by adjusting the 'Gap Size' and 'Divisor' parameters.

The 'Gap Size' adjusts the general size of spacing between glyphs.

The 'Divisor' parameter adjusts the segment size of the underlying background grid.

(below) Overall spacing can be better adjusted with the 'Gap Size' parameter.

(below) Compensation is made for the optical effects of Uppercase characters, by adjusting the 'Tall Glyph Gap Spacing' to adjust the side bearing of the uppercase characters independent of the lowercase.

(below) Further micro-adjustment can be made by adjusting the cadence parameter.

(below) Custom stem positions can be added for those glyphs that need them.
